ScrumLand overview

History of the city

ScrumLand is the culmination of a rich past. To get to know it better, we invite you to take a trip back in time to the origins of the city. From the towers of La Défense to the banks of the Seine, the city of ScrumLand covers nearly 330 hectares, west of Paris. Its fruitful industrial past in the automotive, aeronautics, weapons, radio, inks, perfumes, and its abundant artistic and cultural history give it a strong identity, enriched by the dynamism of the La Défense business district.

Current City

Thanks to its economic fabric (450 local shops, 6,230 companies, 3,280 head offices and 92,800 salaried jobs), ScrumLand has an unemployment rate of 7.3%, one of the weakest in Île-de-France. ScrumLand has however managed to preserve its village spirit for the well-being of its 45,146 inhabitants.

Future of the city

ScrumLand will soon create a municipal swimming pool and a bowling alley to expand its sports activities, delight young and old, and replenish its cash registers thanks to a business finely put together plan.

AIDS vaccine


Thanks to funding from the city, the "JeCherchePleinDeChoses" laboratory found the vaccine against AIDS last October.
